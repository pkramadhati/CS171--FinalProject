Visualizing the Impact of Covid-19 on Travel Industry

BACKGROUND - The Virus That Shut Down the World

Global Distancing

Travel is one of the clearest ways to show how the coronavirus has disrupted the world.

In order to slow down the pandemic, countries issued travel restrictions, lockdown, etc., and therefore, a lot of businesses have been impacted. The project depicts the travel industry from the perspective of public policy, air activities, recreation and provide insights about the future recovery scenarios.
